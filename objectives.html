Our Core Objectives

Sustainable Agriculture

Promoting organic and natural farming methods, reducing chemical use, and providing certification services like soil health cards and organic certification.

Value Addition & Marketing

Enhancing traditional agricultural products (e.g., millets, pulses, herbs) through processing, packaging, and marketing, including G.I. Certified and Organic India/PGS Certified items.

Cottage Industries & Rural Enterprises

Establishing ventures for processing forest produce, making puja materials, vegan products, and developing ecotourism initiatives like homestays and agrotourism.

Animal Welfare

Actively working against animal exploitation and violence, and supporting the protection of stray animals.

Renewable Energy

Focusing on solar energy projects, including solar farms on unused land, to contribute to environmental protection and economic development.

Youth Engagement & Education

Inspiring youth to participate in agriculture through modern technology and entrepreneurial skills, and advocating for vocational subjects in schools.

Financial Inclusion

Offering microcredit, microfinance, and insurance to rural communities and women's Self-Help Groups (SHGs) to promote savings and self-reliance.
